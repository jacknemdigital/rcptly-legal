Common Questions

How do I scan a receipt?

Tap the + button on the home screen, then take a photo of your receipt or import one from your photo library.

How do I turn on return reminders?

Go to Settings and tap Enable Return Reminders to turn on push notifications.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings and tap Delete Account. This will permanently delete your account and all data.

How do I split a receipt?

Open any receipt, tap Split, select the items, enter the contact name, and send a Venmo request.
